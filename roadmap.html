Section 07
Compliance Services Marketplace
A two-sided marketplace connecting VroomX users with independent safety managers and compliance consultants. VroomX earns 10-15% commission on every engagement.
⚗

The Big Idea

Small fleet owners know they need compliance help but can't afford a full-time safety manager ($60-80K/yr). They're already on VroomX seeing red alerts. The marketplace connects their pain (compliance gaps) with the solution (freelance consultants) — and VroomX takes a cut of every transaction.

How It Works

1
User Sees Red Alerts
Dashboard shows compliance gaps: incomplete DQF, expiring medical cards, poor CSA score. Smart banner appears: "Need help? Browse vetted compliance experts."
2
Consultant Matched
User browses marketplace by specialty, location, price, and reviews. Requests a quote. Consultant responds with scope & price. Both agree to engage.
3
VroomX Earns Commission
Payment processed via Stripe Connect. Consultant gets 85-90%. VroomX keeps 10-15%. User rates the consultant. Everyone wins.

Key Strategic Decisions

Build ELD or integrate?
✓ Integrate via API
ELD hardware is capital-intensive and commoditized. Partner with Motive/Samsara APIs instead.
Target owner-ops or small fleets?
✓ Small fleets first (2-10 trucks)
Higher ARPU, more pain points, can afford $39-89/mo. Owner-ops are too price-sensitive.
Raise funding or bootstrap?
✓ Bootstrap to $5K MRR, then decide
Prove PMF first. Raising too early dilutes equity and adds external pressure.
Free tier or trial only?
✓ 14-day trial + free CSA checker
Freemium has high support costs. Free tool as lead gen is more efficient.
Compliance or fleet ops focus?
✓ Stay compliance-focused
This is the moat. Fleet ops is a crowded red ocean dominated by Samsara and Motive.
