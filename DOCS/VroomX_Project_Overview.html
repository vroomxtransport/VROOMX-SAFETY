What is VroomX Safety?

Platform overview and value proposition

The Problem We Solve

Small and mid-sized trucking companies struggle with DOT compliance. They're either:

  • Using spreadsheets and filing cabinets (missing expirations, failing audits)
  • Overpaying for telematics platforms ($30-50/truck) when they only need compliance tools
  • Using outdated software with clunky interfaces and zero AI assistance

Our Solution

VroomX Safety is a compliance-focused SaaS platform that does ONE thing really well: keeps your fleet DOT-compliant and audit-ready.

  • Driver Qualification File (DQF) management
  • Document expiration tracking with alerts
  • CSA score monitoring across all 7 BASICs
  • AI-powered regulation assistant (Claude)
  • DataQ violation challenge drafting
  • Audit-ready reports and checklists

What We're NOT

We deliberately don't try to do everything. We're not:

  • An ELD provider (use your existing one)
  • A GPS tracking platform
  • A dispatch/TMS system
  • A telematics platform
  • An IFTA calculator

We work alongside your existing tools, not replace them.
