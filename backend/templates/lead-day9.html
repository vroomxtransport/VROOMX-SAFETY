Why do CSA scores change so often?

•  New inspections are uploaded to FMCSA databases weekly. A single bad roadside inspection can spike a BASIC by 10+ points overnight.
•  Older violations age out on a rolling 24-month window. Violations from 2+ years ago drop off, which can help -- or hurt if they were clean inspections.
•  Peer group changes shift your relative percentile. Even with no new inspections, your ranking can move as other carriers in your peer group add or remove violations.
•  DataQ challenges by other carriers can shift the curve. If a competitor successfully removes violations, your relative position changes.
